Hebridian bog!\
Hebridian rain!\
What joy it is to be dry again.

Glen, moor and sea,\
a sandy bay,\
a flock of sheep\
with spots of grey,\
a gushing wind and a rare sunray\
will meet you on the Hebridian way.
